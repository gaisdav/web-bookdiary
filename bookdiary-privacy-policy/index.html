Privacy Policy for BookDiary

1. Data Collection and Usage

BookDiary does not collect personal information except for login credentials (such as email) required for authentication using Supabase. This information is stored securely and used solely for user identification and account management.

2. Data Security and Sharing

No sensitive or financial data is collected. Data is used only for providing the core functionality of the app, such as tracking reading progress and managing your book diary. Your data is not shared, sold, or rented to third parties for any reason.

3. Advertising and Analytics

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not engage in deep user tracking or analytics beyond basic app performance monitoring.
